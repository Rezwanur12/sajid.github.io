01 — Introduction

About Me

I am a dedicated and goal-oriented individual currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration in Management Information Systems (MIS) from the University of Dhaka. My interests lie at the intersection of data, design, and business strategy.

I have a passion for machine learning, deep learning, data analysis, and leveraging digital tools to drive innovation. Beyond the technical, I carry a deep affinity for visual communication — particularly through graphic design, mobile photography, and photo editing.

I believe great design is not merely aesthetic — it communicates, it persuades, and it endures. Every composition I create is a conversation between form and intent.
